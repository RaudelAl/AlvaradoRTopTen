2. Sanji

Sanji, the Straw Hat Pirates' chef, is a favorite character for many One Piece fans due to his unique fighting style, chivalrous nature, and the comedic charm he brings to the crew. Introduced as the crew's expert cook, Sanji's dream of finding the All Blue, a legendary sea where fish from all over the world gather, adds a layer of depth to his character beyond his combat abilities. His distinctive fighting style, the Black Leg Style, showcases his agility and powerful kicks, making him a visually dynamic and formidable member of the crew.

3. Naruto

Naruto Uzumaki, the titular character of the series, is a favorite for many due to his inspirational journey, unwavering determination, and the profound themes of acceptance and perseverance that define his character. From the outset, Naruto faces ostracism and loneliness as the container for the Nine-Tails Fox. His journey from being an outcast to earning the respect and admiration of his peers encapsulates a powerful message of resilience and the ability to overcome adversity.

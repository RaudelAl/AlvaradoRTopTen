4. Kakashi

Kakashi Hatake stands out as a favorite character for many Naruto fans due to his enigmatic nature, exceptional skills, and the layers of complexity in his character. From the early stages of the series, where he is introduced as the aloof and mysterious Jonin sensei, to the later revelations about his past and personal struggles, Kakashi's character undergoes significant development. His journey from a lone wolf, haunted by the trauma of losing his friends, to a compassionate leader and mentor adds depth to his narrative.

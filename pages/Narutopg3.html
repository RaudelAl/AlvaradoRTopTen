8. Nagato

Nagato, also known as Pain, emerges as a compelling and tragic figure in the Naruto series, earning a place among potential favorite characters. His backstory, marked by loss, suffering, and the horrors of war, adds a layer of complexity to his character. Nagato's transformation from an idealistic child named Yahiko, striving for peace, to the formidable and ruthless leader of Akatsuki reflects the harsh realities of the ninja world.

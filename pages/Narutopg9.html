2. Sasuke

Sasuke Uchiha is a character with a complex and riveting storyline that captivates many Naruto fans. His journey from a talented and driven young ninja seeking vengeance for the destruction of his clan to a conflicted anti-hero grappling with questions of identity and purpose adds layers of depth to his character. Sasuke's internal struggles, driven by the trauma of his past and the desire for power, make him a compelling and relatable figure.

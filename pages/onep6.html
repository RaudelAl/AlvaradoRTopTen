5. Katakuri

Charlotte Katakuri, the second son of the Charlotte Family and one of the Three Sweet Commanders of the Big Mom Pirates, is a favorite character for many One Piece fans due to his unique Devil Fruit powers, sense of honor, and the unexpected depth of his character. Katakuri's introduction during the Whole Cake Island Arc immediately sets him apart with his intimidating appearance, including his mouth scarf and donut-shaped scarf, and his unmatched observation haki.
